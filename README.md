# VIKI

## An AI Residential Environmental Intelligence Architecture and Reference Implementation

By Antonio Lopez

VIKI is a residential environmental intelligence system built on Home Assistant, distributed environmental agents, local AI inference, and contextual reasoning.

The project explores a different approach to smart homes. Rather than reacting to isolated events, VIKI attempts to maintain an ongoing understanding of occupancy, identity, environmental conditions, and household context before making decisions.

## Topics Covered

- Environmental Intelligence
- Corroborated Reality Modeling
- Distributed Environmental Agents
- Identity and Arrival Intelligence
- Local AI Reasoning
- Environmental Continuity
- Explainable Environmental Intelligence
